My Favourite Human

I've never counted the days, but
it turns out I've come this far.
For you, a good person who is always
fun and annoying at the same time, as
I wrote at the time, even
though your universe cannot
be my universe, I pray for
you always, sincerely. Thank you for
giving me a feeling of
joy that I still haven't found
in other people. Thank you for being
a friend on so many
of my pages, I hope many good things
are waiting for you
on every remaining page.
Anyway happy birthday to you!!!
